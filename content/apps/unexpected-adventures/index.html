About Unexpected Adventures

Discover new places to explore by randomly selecting locations within your areas of interest on a world map.

How to Use

  1. Draw areas on the map where you'd like to explore
  2. Click "Generate Adventures" to create random locations
  3. Or use "Surprise Me" for instant random locations in view
  4. Click markers to see nearby attractions
  5. Use "Go Here" to get navigation directions
